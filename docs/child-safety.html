Kyotee Child Safety Standards

Protecting children and teens who interact with Kyotee is a priority. This declaration outlines the policies and safeguards we maintain to promote a safe environment for younger users.

Eligibility

  • Kyotee is designed for individuals aged 13 and older.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Accounts identified as belonging to children under 13 are removed.
  • Parents or guardians who believe their child has registered in error should contact us immediately so we can assist.

Content and conduct standards

  • We prohibit abusive, sexual, or exploitative content involving minors.
  • Users can report harmful behavior directly within the app. Reports are reviewed promptly and enforcement actions follow our community guidelines.
  • Repeat or severe violations result in account suspension or permanent removal.

Moderation and response

  • Our moderation tooling flags accounts and content for review when potential child safety risks are detected.
  • All reports concerning minors are escalated to our trust and safety team for faster handling.
  • When legally required, we cooperate with law enforcement and child protection authorities.

How parents and guardians can reach us

  1. Email support@kyoteeapp.com with the subject line “Child Safety Request”.
  2. Include the Kyotee username or email involved, a description of your concern, and any supporting screenshots.
  3. We acknowledge all child safety requests within 24 hours and provide status updates until the matter is resolved.
