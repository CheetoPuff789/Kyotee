Privacy Policy

Our Kyotee staff value your privacy and do their best to protect your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle user information when you use our app and services.

We may gather limited information to improve app functionality, such as:

We do not collect extra personal information, and we never track you from app to app or site to site.

How We Use Information

We utilize the information we gather solely for:

Data Storage and Security

We strive to keep your data secure from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Any data we store is encrypted or anonymized whenever we can.

If data is stored locally (on your device) by our application, it is yours to manage. If data is stored on our secure servers, we implement industry-standard measures to protect it.

Sharing Data

We do not disclose, sell, rent, or trade user information to any third parties.

We may share information only where we have a legal obligation to do so or to guarantee the security and integrity of our app.

Your Rights

Depending on where you reside, you have the following rights:

To exercise such rights, please contact us at support@kyoteeapp.com.
