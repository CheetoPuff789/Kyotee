Manage Your Kyotee Data

Use the options below to remove specific content, disconnect contacts, or request help managing your data without deleting your entire account.

Delete content yourself

  • Posts: Open the Kyotee app, go to the post menu (⋮) and tap Delete.
  • Comments: Tap the delete icon next to your comment.
  • Reactions: Tap the reaction again to remove it.
  • Friends: Remove a friend from your profile screen’s Friends section.

Erase additional data

If you want us to clear other information (for example, uploaded avatars or contacts you’ve shared), send an email to support@kyoteeapp.com. Include:

  • The email address tied to your Kyotee account.
  • A summary of the data you want removed (e.g. “delete my avatar” or “disconnect my contacts import”).
  • Any other information that helps us identify the content.
Email support@kyoteeapp.com

Response timeline

  • We acknowledge data-management requests within 72 hours.
  • Confirmed removals are processed as quickly as possible and always within 14 days.
  • We’ll email you once the requested data has been removed.

Need to delete your entire account instead? Visit Kyotee Account Deletion.
