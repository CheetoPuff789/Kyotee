Share Your Kyotee Feedback

We read every message sent to our support team. Use the steps below to share suggestions, bug reports, or general thoughts about your Kyotee experience.

How to contact us

  1. Compose an email to support@kyoteeapp.com.
  2. Set the subject line to “Kyotee Feedback” so we can route your message quickly.
  3. Include any screenshots or details that help us understand your experience.
Email support@kyoteeapp.com

Helpful details to share

  • What you were doing when you ran into an issue or had an idea.
  • Device type and app version (find this in the Kyotee app settings).
  • Steps to reproduce any bugs you discovered.
  • Features you would like to see in future releases.

Our response

  • We acknowledge feedback emails within 3 business days.
  • If a follow-up is needed, our team will start a conversation over email.
  • Urgent issues (like outages or login problems) are prioritized immediately.
